Analisis Spasial

1. Flood Susceptibility Mapping

Overlay analysis: DEM + Slope + Drainage Density + Land Use + Rainfall Distribution

2. Risk Zonation

Weighted overlay menghasilkan 4 kategori: Low, Medium, High, Critical risk zones

3. Buffer Analysis

Multi-ring buffer analysis pada sungai dan area rawan genangan

4. Impact Assessment

Spatial join untuk estimasi populasi dan infrastruktur terdampak
